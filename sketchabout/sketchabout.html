What Do You Wish For On Your Birthday?

Every year, since I can remember,

I've made the same wish on my birthday

when blowing out the candles.

It will never come true,

but I keep on wishing just in case.

You don’t need to know my wish.

You just have to believe in the act of wishing.

Even when it doesn’t make sense.

Especially then.

Dani Quitasol
